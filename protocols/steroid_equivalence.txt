## DEFAULT_ANSWER

To calculate steroid equivalence, provide a supported steroid and dose in mg.

## SELECTION_RULES

selection_mode: none

Supported steroids and reference equivalent doses:

| Steroid | Reference equivalent dose |
|---|---:|
| methylprednisone | 8 mg |
| dexamethasone | 1.5 mg |
| hydrocortisone | 40 mg |
| prednisolone | 10 mg |
| fludrocortisone | 4 mg |

Calculation rule:
- conversion_factor = input_dose_mg / reference_equivalent_dose_for_input_steroid
- equivalent_dose_for_each_steroid = conversion_factor * reference_equivalent_dose_for_that_steroid

If steroid_agent or steroid_dose_mg is missing, ask for the missing input. Do not guess.

## SELECTED_OUTPUTS

### calculated_equivalence

Return equivalent doses for all supported steroids, followed by the generic equivalence table.

### missing_input

Ask for the missing steroid and/or dose in mg.

### unsupported_steroid

State that the steroid is not in the supported equivalence table and list supported steroids.

Generic steroid equivalence table:

| Steroid | Equivalent dose | Glucocorticoid:mineralocorticoid activity | Duration |
|---|---:|---|---|

| prednisolone | 10 mg | 4:0.8 | 12-36 h |
| fludrocortisone | 4 mg | 10:250 | 24 h |

## RESTRICTED_OUTPUTS

Never provide:
- clinical steroid treatment advice outside dose equivalence
- perioperative hydrocortisone supplementation advice from this calculator; use the perioperative steroid guide
- steroid tapering advice
- adrenal insufficiency diagnosis or testing advice
- pediatric dosing
- pregnancy-specific steroid advice
- equivalence for steroids not listed in the source table

## SAFETY_RULES

- This protocol calculates mathematical equivalents only from the source equivalence table.
- Require a steroid name and dose in mg before calculation.
- If the user asks for perioperative steroid supplementation, use the separate perioperative steroid guide.
- Do not infer clinical appropriateness of any converted dose.

## OUTPUT_TEMPLATES
