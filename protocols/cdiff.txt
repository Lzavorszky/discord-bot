### DIAGNOSIS_CHUNK

type: protocol_chunk
selected_items:
- cdiff_diagnosis
chunk_hu:
- Diagnozis:
- Napi 3-nal tobb hig szeklet, Bristol stool chart 5-7.
- Pozitiv C. difficile toxin.
- Megjegyzes: hasmenes nelkuli toxin vagy antigen pozitivitas nem kezelendo.
- Ha hasmenese van es toxin negativ, de antigen pozitiv: ismeteld meg a vizsgalatot, es figyelj ra, hogy a minta gyorsan a mikrobiologiai laborba erjen. Ha a minta sokat all, a toxin lebomolhat.
- A toxin es antigen kimutatas gyors eredmenyt ad. A tenyesztesnek kesobb lesz eredmenye, viszont ez mar biztos eredmenyt ad a toxin szempontjabol, mert nem tud lebomlani.
- Kontakt izolacio es szappanos kezmosas szukseges.
- Az izolaciot a hasmenes megszuneset koveto 48 oraig kell fenntartani; ezt kovetoen megszuntetheto, negativ vizsgalat nem szukseges.
chunk_en:
- Diagnosis:
- More than 3 loose stools per day, Bristol stool chart 5-7.
- Positive C. difficile toxin.
- Note: toxin or antigen positivity without diarrhea should not be treated.
- If diarrhea is present and toxin is negative but antigen is positive: repeat the test and ensure the sample reaches the microbiology lab quickly. If the sample stands too long, toxin may degrade.
- Toxin and antigen detection gives a rapid result. Culture results arrive later, but toxin status is more secure from culture because toxin cannot degrade in the same way.
- Contact isolation and soap-and-water handwashing are required.
- Keep isolation until 48 hours after diarrhea stops; after that it may be stopped, and a negative test is not required.
forbidden:
- do not treat toxin or antigen positivity without diarrhea

### TREATMENT_CHUNK

type: protocol_chunk
selected_items:
- vancomycin
- metronidazole
- tigecycline

chunk_hu:
- Kezeles:
- NG vancomycin 4x125 mg.
- Sulyos fertozesben, es ha NG ut nem megbizhato: + metronidazol.
- Ha lehet, allitsd meg a konvencionalis antibiotikumokat.
- Tigecyclin is hatekony; kulonosen toxikus megacolon vagy szeptikus allapot eseten jon szoba. Konzultalj infektologussal.
- Terapiarezisztens esetekben fidaxomicin beszerezheto, illetve szeklettranszplantacio merul fel. Szt. Laszlo Infektologiai Centrummal konzultacio szukseges.
- Probiotikumok nem hatekonyak.
- Hossz: minimum 10 nap; illetve a hasmenes megszunte utan +48 oraig.
- 10 napnal hosszabb vagy visszatero fertozes eseten hosszabb kezelesre vagy alternativ strategiara lehet szukseg; konzultalj infektologussal.
chunk_en:
- Treatment:
- NG vancomycin 4x125 mg.
- In severe infection, and if the NG route is unreliable: add metronidazole.
- Stop conventional antibiotics if possible.
- Tigecycline is also effective; especially in toxic megacolon or septic state. Consult infectious diseases.
- In therapy-resistant cases, fidaxomicin may be obtained, and fecal microbiota transplantation may be considered. Consultation with Szt. Laszlo Infectious Diseases Center is required.
- Probiotics are not effective.
- Duration: minimum 10 days; and until 48 hours after diarrhea stops.
- If treatment longer than 10 days is needed or infection recurs, longer treatment or an alternative strategy may be needed; consult infectious diseases.
forbidden:
- do not invent recurrence regimens
- do not add probiotics as effective therapy

## INFO_BLOCKS

### diagnostic_notes

Do not treat toxin or antigen positivity without diarrhea.
If diarrhea is present with toxin-negative but antigen-positive result, repeat testing and send the sample rapidly to microbiology because toxin may degrade if the sample stands.
Contact isolation and soap-and-water handwashing are required until 48 hours after diarrhea resolves.

### treatment_notes

Stop conventional antibiotics if possible.
For toxic megacolon, septic state, therapy resistance, recurrence, fidaxomicin access, or fecal microbiota transplantation, consult infectious diseases as specified by the source.
Probiotics are not effective.

## RESTRICTED_OUTPUTS

- Do not treat toxin or antigen positivity without diarrhea.
- Do not invent treatment for recurrence or therapy resistance beyond source-supported consultation, fidaxomicin access, and fecal microbiota transplantation consideration.
- Do not recommend probiotics as effective.
- Do not omit source isolation instructions when answering diagnosis.
- If the user does not choose diagnosis or treatment, ask which chunk they want.

- Return the chosen chunk whole.
