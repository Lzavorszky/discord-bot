## INFO_BLOCKS

### source_scope

This protocol contains only the dosing, preparation, administration time, and pump settings from the uploaded DOCX table.
The source explicitly names GFR 30-90, GFR <30, anuria/IHD, and CRRT rows.
The source does not define a numeric cutoff for Step-up dose.

### administration

Use only the preparation and pump settings listed in DEFAULT_ANSWER or SELECTED_OUTPUTS.
Do not invent alternative dilutions, infusion durations, or pump rates.

## RESTRICTED_OUTPUTS

Never provide:
- cefiderocol dosing outside the uploaded table rows
- numeric GFR cutoff mapping for Step-up dose
- Step-up dose unless TDM suggests low levels
- alternative antibiotics, duration, toxicity management, or indication advice from this protocol

## SAFETY_RULES

- This is an open dosing-table protocol: broad drug requests may return DEFAULT_ANSWER.
- IHD, CRRT, GFR 30-90, and GFR <30 can be selected because they are explicit in the uploaded row.
- If GFR is above 90 without an explicit Step-up/TDM indication, show DEFAULT_ANSWER rather than selecting Step-up dose.
