## DEFAULT_ANSWER

### HU

Amikacin dosing table:

TDM: target trough <10 mg/L. Give at 10:00 where possible and check trough with morning labs. If given after 18:00, skip the next day's dose/level. If trough is toxic, skip the next dose; do not routinely reduce the first dose.

|---|---|---:|

| IHD | IHD | do not use |
