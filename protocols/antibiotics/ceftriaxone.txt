## DEFAULT_ANSWER

### HU

Ceftriaxone dosing table:

No dose reduction is required for renal or liver dysfunction.

| Tier | When | Dose |
|---|---|---:|
| Loading | start of therapy | 2 g IV bolus once, then continue scheduled dosing |
| Normal | any renal or liver function | 1 x 2 g IV bolus |
| High-dose | CNS infection, septic shock, or albumin <30 g/L | 2 x 2 g IV bolus |
