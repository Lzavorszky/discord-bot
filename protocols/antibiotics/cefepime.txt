## DEFAULT_ANSWER

### HU

Cefepime dosing table:

| Tier | When | Daily dose | Administration | Preparation | Pump |
|---|---|---:|---|---|---|
| Normal | source Normal bucket | 4 g/day | one 2 g syringe over 12 h | 2 g in 50 mL NaCl 0.9% -> 40 mg/mL | 4.2 mL/h |
| Severe renal dysfunction | source severe renal dysfunction bucket | 1 g/day | source note: 2 x 1 dose / 12 h | reduced-dose preparation note below | 4.2 mL/h |
| CRRT | CRRT | 3 g/day | one 2 g syringe over 16 h | 2 g in 50 mL NaCl 0.9% -> 40 mg/mL | 3.2 mL/h |
| Step-up dose | only if TDM suggests low levels | 8 g/day | one 2 g syringe over 6 h | 2 g in 50 mL NaCl 0.9% -> 40 mg/mL | 8.4 mL/h |

Reduced-dose preparation note from source: dissolve the 1 g ampoule in 20 mL NaCl 0.9%, withdraw half (10 mL), dilute further to 50 mL, giving a 500 mg/50 mL syringe over 12 h. Label the remaining ampoule with date/name, refrigerate, and prepare the second 50 mL syringe after 12 h.

The uploaded table does not define numeric GFR cutoffs for Normal, severe renal dysfunction, or Step-up dose.
