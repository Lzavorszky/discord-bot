## DEFAULT_ANSWER

### HU

Cefepime dosing table:

| Tier | When | Daily dose | Administration |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Loading | start of therapy | 2 g once | start continuous infusion immediately |
| Normal | GFR 20+ | 4 g/day | 2 g/50 mL, 4.2 mL/h |
| Severe AKI | GFR <20 or IHD | 1 g/day | 0.5 g/50 mL, 4.2 mL/h |
| CRRT | CRRT | 3 g/day | 2 g/50 mL, 3.2 mL/h |

| Step-up dose | low levels | 6 g/day | 2 g/50 mL, 6.4 mL/h |

### EN

Cefepime dosing table:

| Tier | When | Daily dose | Administration |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Loading | start of therapy | 2 g once | start continuous infusion immediately |
| Normal | GFR 20+ | 4 g/day | 2 g/50 mL, 4.2 mL/h |
| Severe AKI | GFR <20 or IHD | 1 g/day | 0.5 g/50 mL, 4.2 mL/h |
| CRRT | CRRT | 3 g/day | 2 g/50 mL, 3.2 mL/h |

| Step-up dose | low levels | 6 g/day | 2 g/50 mL, 6.4 mL/h |

Reduced-dose preparation: dissolve 1 g in 20 mL NaCl 0.9%, withdraw 10 mL, dilute to 50 mL for a 0.5 g/50 mL syringe.
