### administration

Use glucose or water for injection for fosfomycin preparation; the source explicitly says not saline.
Use only the preparation and pump settings listed in DEFAULT_ANSWER or SELECTED_OUTPUTS.
Do not invent alternative dilutions, infusion durations, or pump rates.

- fosfomycin dosing outside the uploaded table rows
