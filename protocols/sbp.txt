SBP protokoll:

Diagnozis = paracentezis.

Kuldd:
- mikrobiologia: ten mL ascites hemokulturas palackban
- kemia: LDH, glukoz, bilirubin, amilaz, albumin, feherversejtszam
- sejtszam
- citologia
- ascites vergaz gepbe, ha makroszkoposan tiszta

### EN

SBP protocol:

Diagnosis = paracentesis.

Send:
- microbiology: ten mL ascites in a blood culture bottle
- chemistry: LDH, glucose, bilirubin, amylase, albumin, white blood cell count
- cell count
- cytology
- ascites into blood gas machine if macroscopically clear

Diagnosis: neutrophil granulocytes above 500/uL, low pH, low glucose and high lactate, high albumin gradient.

Treatment from the abdominal infection table: ceftriaxone. For detailed ceftriaxone dosing use the ceftriaxone protocol.
