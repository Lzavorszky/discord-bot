## DEFAULT_ANSWER

Proposed primary AVA calculation: LVOT diameter plus LVOT VTI plus aortic valve VTI. LVOT diameter may be in mm or cm; VTI values may be in mm or cm.

## SELECTION_RULES

selection_mode: none

Primary continuity-equation calculation:
- Convert LVOT diameter to cm if supplied in mm.
- Convert LVOT VTI and AV VTI to cm if supplied in mm.
- If LVOT CSA is supplied directly in cm2, use it directly.
- If LVOT CSA is not supplied, calculate LVOT_CSA_cm2 = pi * (LVOT_diameter_cm / 2)^2.
- AVA_cm2 = LVOT_CSA_cm2 * LVOT_VTI_cm / AV_VTI_cm.
- Dimensionless_index = LVOT_VTI_cm / AV_VTI_cm.
- If BSA_m2 is supplied: Indexed_AVA_cm2_m2 = AVA_cm2 / BSA_m2.

Optional simplified velocity calculation:
- If LVOT VTI and AV VTI are not available but LVOT Vmax and AV Vmax are supplied, calculate velocity_ratio = LVOT_Vmax / AV_Vmax.
- If LVOT CSA is also available, simplified_AVA_cm2 = LVOT_CSA_cm2 * LVOT_Vmax / AV_Vmax.
- Prefer the VTI continuity-equation AVA when VTI measurements are available.

## SELECTED_OUTPUTS

### calculated_ava

Return normalized inputs, LVOT CSA, AVA in cm2, and dimensionless index.

### calculated_ava_indexed

Return normalized inputs, LVOT CSA, AVA in cm2, dimensionless index, BSA, and indexed AVA in cm2/m2.

### calculated_velocity_ratio

Return velocity ratio, and simplified AVA only if LVOT CSA is available. State that the VTI continuity equation is preferred when available.

### missing_input

Ask for LVOT VTI, AV VTI, and either LVOT diameter or LVOT CSA. If the user wants indexed AVA, ask for BSA.

### unsupported_units

Ask the user to resend diameters in mm or cm, VTIs in mm or cm, velocities in m/s or cm/s, and BSA in m2.

## INFO_BLOCKS

### proposed_calculation

Use the VTI continuity equation as the primary AVA calculation: AVA = LVOT CSA * LVOT VTI / AV VTI. Also report dimensionless index as LVOT VTI / AV VTI. If BSA is supplied, AVA can be indexed to BSA.

### quality_notes

The LVOT diameter is squared and is a major source of measurement error. The standard continuity equation assumes a circular LVOT, laminar LVOT flow, aligned Doppler, and compatible measurement levels. The result is an effective valve area, not direct anatomic planimetry.

## RESTRICTED_OUTPUTS

Never provide:
- definitive aortic stenosis diagnosis from AVA alone
- intervention recommendations
- valve replacement recommendations
- interpretation of discordant AVA, gradient, velocity, stroke volume, or ejection fraction unless another active protocol specifies it
- assumptions for missing LVOT diameter, LVOT CSA, LVOT VTI, AV VTI, BSA, or velocities

## SAFETY_RULES

- Prefer VTI continuity-equation AVA when LVOT VTI and AV VTI are available.
- If both diameter-derived LVOT CSA and directly supplied LVOT CSA are present and disagree, show both only if the user asks; otherwise ask which LVOT CSA to use.
- If units are ambiguous, ask for clarification rather than guessing.
- Do not grade stenosis severity unless the user asks and the answer is limited to formula thresholds explicitly present in this protocol.
